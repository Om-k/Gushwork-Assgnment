Built to Last. Engineered to Perform.

From bulk bags to technical threads, Mangalam delivers precision solutions for every stage of your piping infrastructure.

Superior Chemical Resistance

HDPE pipes resist a wide range of chemicals, acids, and alkalis. Unlike metal pipes, they won't corrode, rust, or scale, ensuring pure water quality and extended service life in aggressive environments.

Exceptional Flexibility & Durability

Our HDPE pipes flex under ground movement without cracking, making them ideal for seismic zones and challenging terrains. Built for decades of reliable performance under high stress.

Leak-Proof Fusion Welding

Butt fusion and electrofusion joints create a monolithic, fully leak-proof pipeline. No gaskets, no rubber rings — the joint is as strong as the pipe itself.

Cost-Effective Long-Term Solution

Lower installation costs, minimal maintenance, and 50+ year lifespan make HDPE the most economical piping choice over time compared to steel, ductile iron, or PVC alternatives.

Environmentally Sustainable

100% recyclable at end of life and manufactured with lower carbon footprint than metal alternatives. HDPE contributes to green infrastructure and sustainable development goals.

Certified Quality Assurance

Every pipe is manufactured to IS 4984, ISO 4427, and ASTM D3035 standards. Our in-house lab conducts rigorous testing at every production stage for guaranteed performance.

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

Our twin-screw extruders ensure homogeneous melt, consistent throughput, and tight dimensional tolerances across all pipe sizes.

  • Temperature-controlled die heads
  • Real-time melt pressure monitoring

Controlled Cooling

Multi-stage cooling baths crystallise the polymer structure uniformly, locking in the mechanical properties for long-term performance.

  • Staged temperature reduction
  • Residual stress elimination

Precision Sizing

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • ±0.1mm dimensional accuracy
  • Ovality controlled per ISO 11922

Quality Control Testing

In-house lab performs hydrostatic, tensile, and impact tests on every batch to ensure compliance with IS, ISO, and ASTM standards.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • MFR and density checks

Pipe Marking & Identification

Every pipe is indelibly marked with diameter, SDR, pressure rating, standard, and batch number for full traceability across the supply chain.

  • Inline inkjet printing
  • Full batch traceability

Precision Cutting

Automated saw cutting delivers burr-free, square ends for reliable fusion welding. Custom lengths available on request.

  • Standard lengths: 6m & 12m
  • Custom lengths on request

Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are bundled, strapped, and labelled for safe transit. Coils are wound and crated to prevent deformation during long-distance shipping.

  • Bundled & strapped for transit
  • PAN India & export dispatch
